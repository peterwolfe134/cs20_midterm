Visit

Welcome to our booking page! Here, you can schedule a unique visit to our cat café and enjoy quality time with our friendly cats. Select from various session types to find the perfect experience for you, from cozy meet-and-greets to private events. We look forward to sharing our space and furry friends with you!

It is necessary to be aware of our rules before coming in to the cafe. This is for the safety of all the visitors as well as our cats. Failure to abide by the rules may result in a ban from the cafe. Click here to access our rules.

Basic Cat Lounge Visit

Enjoy a 50-minute session in the lounge with our cats for individuals aged 15 and above. Library and cafe are accessible before and after the session.

Price: $20 per person

Basic Group Appointment (4+ People)

Bring your friends and enjoy a group session with our furry companions.

Price: $65, up to 6 people

2 Hour Paint with Cats

Unleash your creativity while enjoying the company of our playful cats. Acrylic and watercolor paints provided.

Price: $40 per person

Saturday Study Special

Join us on Thursdays for a 1.5-hour session with complimentary pastries and coffee of your choice!

Price: $30 per person

Private Party Reservation

2-hour private event for up to 10 guests, complete with a reserved space, snacks, and beverages.

Price: $150 (includes drinks and snacks for guests)

Cat Adoption Consultation

30-minute one-on-one consultation with staff about adopting a cat from the café, including a Q&A session and support on caring for a new cat.

Price: Free
