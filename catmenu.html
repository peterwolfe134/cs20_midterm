🐾 Meows and Mugs Menu 🐾

Where every sip, bite, and purr is pure joy.

Specialty Coffee & Espresso ☕

  • • Catpuccino – Creamy cappuccino with a light dusting of cocoa paw prints.
  • • Meowcchiato – Rich espresso with a swirl of house-made caramel.
  • • Whisker’s Brew – A velvety blend of oat milk latte and cinnamon, perfect for a purr-fect morning.
  • • Cold Brew Claw – Smooth cold brew steeped for 18 hours, served with optional vanilla cream.
  • • Espurrresso Shot – Triple-shot espresso for those needing a feline-powered boost.
  • • French Purr-ess Coffee – Served in individual presses for an elegant, DIY touch.

Teas & Infusions 🍵

  • • Chamomile Catnap – Calming chamomile with hints of lavender.
  • • Feline Forest Green Tea – Organic sencha with mint and jasmine.
  • • Earl Grey Pawfect – Classic Earl Grey with creamy steamed milk and vanilla.
  • • Purrsian Chai Latte – Spiced black tea with steamed milk and a drizzle of honey.
  • • Strawberry & Hibiscus Meowjito (Iced) – A fruity herbal tea with mint and lime.

Signature Pastries & Sweets 🍰

  • • Pawberry Tart – Fresh berries, silky custard, and a buttery shortcrust.
  • • Whisker’s Macarons – Assortment of premium macarons in lavender, matcha, and rose.
  • • Chocolate Catnip Brownie – A decadent, catnip-themed chocolate treat (for humans only!).
  • • Cat Paw Donuts – Mini donuts shaped like paws, dusted with powdered sugar or glazed.
  • • Kitten’s Eclair – Choux pastry filled with vanilla cream and topped with a chocolate paw design.

Savory Bites & Brunch 🥪

  • • Cheddar Purrfection Sandwich – A grilled cheese masterpiece with aged cheddar and brioche.

  • • Meowin’ Mushroom Quiche – Savory quiche filled with mushrooms, goat cheese, and herbs.

  • • Purrito Bites – Catnip-infused treats wrapped in chicken or salmon (for your feline friends).

Seasonal Offerings 🍂
