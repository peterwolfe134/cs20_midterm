Adopt

At Meows & Mugs, we believe that every cat deserves a loving home. Here, you'll find a diverse array of feline friends, each with their own unique personalities and stories. Whether you're looking for a playful companion, a gentle cuddle buddy, or a curious explorer, our shelter is filled with cats eager to find their forever families. Browse through our adorable profiles, then come in and discover the purrfect match for you!

To adopt one of our wonderful cats, you’ll need to visit our shelter in person to meet them and fill out an adoption application form below.

Our Cats

Nimbu

Nimbu is a soft, snuggly cloud of love! This affectionate feline adores being held and will nuzzle right into your arms. With her playful spirit, Nimbu keeps things lively while bringing a warm touch of joy to any home.

Jinx

Jinx is a spirited ball of energy who brings excitement wherever he goes! Always up for a game of chase, this playful kitty is both social and adventurous. Jinx is looking for an active family who can keep up with his playful antics.

Quibble

Quibble is a sophisticated lady with an air of mystery. While she enjoys her independence, this elegant feline loves a good cuddle session when the mood strikes. Quibble is perfect for a tranquil home where she can explore her curiosity at her own pace.

Marigold

Marigold is a golden gem with a luxurious coat and a gentle soul. Though she may be a bit older, she exudes grace and charm. Marigold enjoys peaceful moments, gentle pets, and is searching for a loving home to enjoy her twilight years.

Simba

Meet Simba, the king of the jungle (or at least your living room)! He’s a spirited cat who loves to play fetch and climb to new heights. Simba is very social and thrives on attention. He’s looking for an active family who can keep up with his adventurous spirit.

Moxie

Moxie is a spunky little explorer with a flair for the dramatic! Her adventurous spirit leads her to investigate every corner of her environment. With her playful antics and affectionate nature, Moxie will steal your heart and keep you entertained for hours.

Tater Tot

Tater Tot is a fluffy bundle of joy who loves to bring smiles to everyone around her. Her playful nature makes her the life of the party, and she adores interactive playtime with her humans. Tater Tot is ready to find her forever home where she can share her warmth and happiness!.

Pudding

Pudding is an adorable and playful kitten with a sweet disposition. She loves to leap around and chase after her toys, and her goofy personality will bring endless joy to your home. Pudding is eager to find her forever family and create beautiful memories together!

Ari

Ari is a cheerful little whirlwind with a playful attitude! He loves to dart around and explore, always ready for a new adventure. With his bubbly personality, Ari is eager to find a fun-loving family to share his energy and affection.
