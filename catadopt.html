Adopt

At Meows & Mugs, we believe that every cat deserves a loving home. Here, you'll find a diverse array of feline friends, each with their own unique personalities and stories. Whether you're looking for a playful companion, a gentle cuddle buddy, or a curious explorer, our shelter is filled with cats eager to find their forever families. Browse through our adorable profiles, then come in and discover the purrfect match for you!

To adopt one of our wonderful cats, you’ll need to visit our shelter in person to meet them and fill out an adoption application form below.

Our Cats

Bingus

The bestest little goobie woobie ever, Bingus is a Sphynx cat who loves snuggling at night. His hairless charm makes him the ultimate cuddle buddy.

Patrick

Meet Patrick, a silly and delusional orange tabby who does funny things. He’s the comedic genius you didn’t know you needed.

Mr. Fluffyshmufulufagus

With fur that feels like 1,000 cashmere sweaters, Mr. Fluffyshmufulufagus is a super fluffy Maine Coon who loves being pet. He’s basically a walking cloud.

E.P.

Always eepy, E. P. is a sleepy Ragdoll who loves to sleep day and night. Napping isn’t just a hobby; it’s his lifestyle.

Charles

Pretentious with an inflated opinion of himself, Charles is a British Shorthair who challenges every dog—and sometimes wins. He believes he’s the king of the animal kingdom.

Gilbert

A good guy all around, Gilbert is a very athletic Bengal cat and the captain of the high school football team. Yes, you read that right.

Hungie

Hungie is a hungry Norwegian Forest Cat who eats anything. He’ll jump for joy at the sight of food—and then devour it all.

Hermione

Looking just like a certain wizard, this Siberian cat is very smart and studies hard. Previous owner reports a high incidence of magic tricks.

Barbie

Barbie is a pink Persian cat (yes, pink!) who is the protagonist of her world. She can do anything—because she’s Barbie.
